Vision

Why Old Paintings?



At Hogans Old Pictures, we believe that art is not just decoration—it's a bridge to the past, a reflection of history, culture, and personal stories. We specialize in recreating and restoring old paintings because we are passionate about preserving the timeless beauty and emotional depth these works carry. Every painting holds a story, whether it's a famous masterpiece or a cherished family heirloom. By restoring or recreating these pieces, we help people reconnect with the past, revive memories, and appreciate the artistry that endures through time.

Our mission is to keep this heritage alive. Over time, many old paintings fade, crack, or become damaged, and without proper care, they risk being lost forever. We don't just sell art—we preserve history. Through our recreations, we make it possible for anyone to enjoy the beauty of these timeless works in their homes or businesses. For those who have personal connections to older pieces, our restoration services breathe new life into these treasures, allowing them to be enjoyed for many more years.

We sell old pictures because they represent the stories, traditions, and memories that define who we are. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to bring a piece of that history into their lives. Whether it's a replica of a famous painting or the restoration of a sentimental portrait, we are committed to ensuring that these artworks continue to inspire, connect, and endure for generations to come.


Why mainly local?

At Hogans Old Pictures, we primarily focus on local work because we value the importance of community and the personal connections that come with our craft. Working locally allows us to offer a more hands-on, personalized experience for our clients, meeting with them face-to-face to discuss their needs and preferences in detail. This ensures that each recreation or restoration is tailored specifically to the customer's vision.

By concentrating on local projects, we can provide a higher level of care when handling and delivering artwork. Whether it's personally transporting pieces or ensuring delicate restorations are completed with precision, being nearby allows us to build trust and ensure that each artwork is treated with the utmost respect. Art, especially vintage works or family heirlooms, is deeply personal, and our proximity helps us maintain a strong connection with our clients throughout the process.

Additionally, focusing locally helps us manage our time and resources more effectively, ensuring that every project receives the attention it deserves. This allows us to deliver high-quality results in a timely manner, while also providing exceptional customer service. Our commitment to the local community also reflects our belief in preserving and supporting the artistic and cultural heritage of the area we serve.

While we occasionally take on projects from clients outside the region, our local focus allows us to maintain the personal touch and craftsmanship that define Hogans Old Pictures.
